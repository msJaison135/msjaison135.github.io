About

I’m a DevOps Engineer with over 7 years of experience in designing and implementing solutions across diverse tech stacks. My journey spans various industries where I’ve worked on building and maintaining scalable, efficient, and secure infrastructures.

My expertise covers everything from automating complex deployments, managing cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, Azure), and orchestrating containers (Docker, Kubernetes), to configuring CI/CD pipelines with tools like Jenkins and GitHub Actions. I’m passionate about optimizing workflows, ensuring high availability, and helping teams ship faster while improving reliability.

Some of the technologies and tools I work with include:

  • Cloud Platforms: AWS, GCP, Azure
  • Containers: Docker, Kubernetes
  • Automation: Chef, Ansible, Terraform
  • Monitoring & Logging: Grafana, Prometheus, ELK
  • Scripting: Python, Bash, Shell, Ruby

Aside from my technical pursuits, I’m also a problem-solver who enjoys working closely with teams to deliver impactful solutions. Whether it’s troubleshooting complex issues or helping developers streamline their workflows, I find great satisfaction in fostering collaboration and continuous improvement.
